Erynsketia

Long long ago there was much joy and excitement in the land of Erynsketia! People enjoyed epic adventures to around the lands claiming riches to benifit everyone around them. There were 5 different realms that were ruled by great kings that had amazing powers. They all worked together to keep out the darkness.

One fateful day darkness began to seep into all corners of the land. Violence began to develop and the little boglings began to act in irratic and crazy manners. It was revealed that a dark sorcerer was trying to harness the powers of all of these great kings to rule the land.

Unfortunately one by one each of the great Kings began to lose hope as the power of the sorcerer was beyond them. All at once they disappeared from off the face of the earth along with their powers, interestingly enough the dark sorcerer also vanished.

Since then all nations have been trying to recupperate from these attacks, but there is a great darkness that has been left in the land with violence all over.

The Argonians, Boglings, Stalwarts, Zudenees, and Burmecians need to be brought back together in harmony, and gain those powers back in order to remove the darkness from the land.
